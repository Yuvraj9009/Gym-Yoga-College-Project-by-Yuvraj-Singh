Health Tips & Diet Plan

Simple Diet Tips

Having a good diet is just as important as working out. Here are some simple guidelines:

  • Protein Foods: Include chicken breast, eggs, fish, paneer, tofu, and lentils in your diet. Protein is essential for muscle repair.
  • Hydration: Drink at least 3-4 liters of water every day. Staying hydrated keeps your energy levels up.
  • Balanced Diet: Do not just focus on one macronutrient. Eat complex carbohydrates (like brown rice, oats), healthy fats (nuts, avocados), and plenty of vegetables.
  • Avoid Sugar: Reduce intake of processed foods and added sugars.

Health Tips for Beginners

  • Start slow. Don't push your body too hard on the first day.
  • Always perform a 10-minute warm-up before any workout.
  • Consistency is more important than intensity. Try to work out 3-4 times a week regularly.
  • Get at least 7-8 hours of sleep. Muscles recover while you rest.
  • Listen to your body. If you feel sharp pain, stop the exercise immediately.
