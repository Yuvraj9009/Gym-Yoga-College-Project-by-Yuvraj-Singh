Yoga Poses for Peace & Wellness

Tree Pose (Vrksasana)

Benefits: Improves balance, strengthens thighs, calves, and ankles.

Steps: Stand tall. Place your right foot high up on your left thigh. Bring your hands together in front of your chest. Hold and breathe deeply.

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

Benefits: Stretches the chest, strengthens the spine, and relieves stress.

Steps: Lie on your stomach. Place hands under shoulders. Inhale and slowly lift your chest off the floor, keeping elbows slightly bent.

Child’s Pose (Balasana)

Benefits: Gently stretches hips and thighs, calms the brain, and helps relieve fatigue.

Steps: Kneel on the floor. Sit on your heels and bend forward, stretching arms out in front of you. Rest your forehead on the floor.

Lotus Pose (Padmasana)

Benefits: Opens the hips, stretches the ankles, and calms the mind.

Steps: Sit with legs extended. Bend right knee and place right foot on left thigh. Repeat with the left leg. Keep spine straight.
