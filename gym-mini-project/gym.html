Gym Exercises

Without Equipment

Push-ups

Technique: Place hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until chest touches the floor, then push back up. Keep your back straight.

Bodyweight Squats

Technique: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Lower your hips as if sitting in a chair, keeping your chest up. Push through heels to return to start.

Plank

Technique: Rest your forearms on the floor. Keep your body straight from head to heels. Engage your core and hold the position.

With Equipment

Bench Press

Technique: Lie back on a flat bench. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width. Lower the bar to your chest, then press it back up.

Dumbbell Curl

Technique: Stand holding a dumbbell in each hand. Keep elbows close to your torso. Curl the weights while contracting your biceps.

Deadlift

Technique: Stand with mid-foot under the barbell. Bend over and grab the bar. Keep back straight, lift chest, and stand up with the weight.
